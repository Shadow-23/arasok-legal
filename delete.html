Deleting your Arasok data

Last updated: May 13, 2026

Arasok stores all of your data locally on your device. PDSJ Labs does not operate any server that stores your training history, profile information, or account data. This means deleting your data is something you do directly on your phone, and the process is immediate.

How to delete your data

  1. If you signed in with Google, open Arasok, go to Profile, and tap Sign out. This clears the cached Google identity from the app.
  2. Uninstall Arasok from your device (long-press the app icon → Uninstall, or use the Play Store → Manage apps → Uninstall). Uninstalling removes the local database that holds your workouts, body measurements, profile information, and any cached photos.

That's it. Because nothing is stored on a server, no separate request is necessary.

What data this covers

  • Workout history (exercises, sets, reps, weights, notes, sessions)
  • Profile information (display name, age, height, weight, goals, injury history)
  • Body measurements
  • Avatar photo if you added one
  • Cached Google account identifier, email, name, and profile photo URL (if you used Google Sign-In)

Google account data

Signing out in Arasok does not delete your Google account itself — your Google account is managed by Google, not by Arasok. If you want to revoke Arasok's access to your Google profile information, visit myaccount.google.com/permissions and remove Arasok from the list of apps with access.

Need help?

If you uninstalled the app but want written confirmation that no data is retained, email pdjstewart@gmail.com. You'll get a response within a few business days.
